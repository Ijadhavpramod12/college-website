About Us

Shri Chhatrapati Shivaji College was established in June 1959; it was immediately after the inception of Marathwada University.
This was first college in old Osmanabad district to start science faculty. Expensive science faculty was a great difficulty, but our venerable members were well determined to keep it going since the beginning.
The college has been named after the great warrior of Maharashtra Chhatrapati Shivaji.
